XIXOIO is a multiple module fintech platform.

XIXOIO's various product line consists of 11 solutions, ranging from remittance services for fiat and cash transactions, AML compliance CloudWare gateway, to banking solutions, legal services, stock exchange and banking cryptocurrency association think-tank.

Their main goal is to develop a fully compliant end-to-end solution for tokenization of companies and security token investments. It aims to integrate all services will be the IPCO – Initial Public Coin Offering platform https://www.ipco.io/.

The IPCO platform is solving all the legal, regulatory and compliance issues, up to the point when the funds are raised, investors shares are written into business registers and tokens are deployed on the secondary markets – STO exchanges – for accredited investors.

The TopMonks Blockchain team was approached at the time where their own IPCO was in the preparation. In just 3 days we have managed to draft the complete token issuance architecture.

Rapidly prepared innovative solution tailored for the customer's needs on one hand and on the other, that the client is capable of quick decision taking has established a trust between both parties.

Backend is handling the token pool and sale contract. All is protected by a multisig solution with optional hardware ledger protection.

Token pool is a decentralized solution for temporary holding of tokens for users without prior knowledge of crypto.
