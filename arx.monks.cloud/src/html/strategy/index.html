We seek to Invest in Central European companies with EBITDAs of at least € 1 million that are valued between € 10 million and € 50 million in enterprise value (“EV”)

KEY Transaction types

Succession Situations of Family Businesses

This is our favorite type of transaction. The owners would like to divest entirely from the business in order to pursue other opportunities, or partially divest while retaining a shareholding alongside ARX.

Corporate
Divestitures

A corporation seeking to divest a Central European subsidiary.

Management Buyouts (MBO)  / Buy-ins (MBI)

Managers of a company would like to become co-owners of the business, or an outside management team would like to acquire a company with our financial support.

Growth Equity

A company seeking capital to strengthen its market position, acquire a complementary business or fund a significant capex investment

Financial Restructuring

A company seeking capital to help get through a period of financial or operational stress
