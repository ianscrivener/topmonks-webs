Mission statement

Our core objective is to form successful partnerships with owners, managers and other stakeholders to realise the full potential of our portfolio companies while achieving superior returns for the firm‘s investors and partner entrepreneurs.

Background history

With more than € 300 million raised in four funds, ARX has become a leading private equity firm in its segment operating in the Central European region. We’re proud of our 20-year track-record investing in and supporting the growth of mid-sized companies.
