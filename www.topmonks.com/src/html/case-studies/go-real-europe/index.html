Story

Founder David Manley approached us with an idea to automate his business, previously operated via his magical Excel spreadsheet. David had tremendous experience in the travel industry, providing customers with an on-demand tailor-made itinerary for traveling to Europe. Customer satisfaction was extremely high, but scalability was not. TopMonks fixed that.

The GRE team becomes an expert on every destination they sell, combining the knowledge of locals with their own first-hand experiences. That expertise is input into a uniquely structured database, enabling GRE’s proprietary algorithm to instantly create a perfect travel itinerary. The itinerary navigates travelers through a stress-free trip, making sure they never get lost or waste time. Even the most complex trips become a seamless experience.

Costumers pick

Customers pick their preferred dates, cities, interests, and activities.

Custom itinerary

A unique, custom itinerary with the overall plan, transportation, tours, transfers and hotels bookings is automatically generated.

The itinerary reflects

The itinerary reflects the preferences of the travelers while also accounting for seasonal activities, date restrictions, and transportation schedules.

The itinerary provides smart navigation

The itinerary provides smart navigation for self-guided travel, one-step package booking, and the opportunity to experience special activities such as symphony concerts, cycling trips, private guides, cable cars to mountain summits, and much more.

Details information

All the details and information the traveler needs for his particular trip are at her fingertips.

The next big thing?

GRE has grown tremendously, with $2.3M in sales already this year, putting them on track to double 2017’s total. Recently TopMonks helped GRE raise $200k to expand into new locations, and they are now raising another $1.5M to expand globally. This could be the next unicorn, and we are thrilled to be a part of this venture.
