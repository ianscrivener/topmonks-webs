24Cryptex.com

24Cryptex is the trading platform with crypto assets based on binary options. Blockchain team of TopMonks delivered backend and frontend solution, design of the platform and usability tests.

About project

Our team was asked to review the 24Cryptex trading app which had problems with usability, overall look and feel of their platform.

UX Designer: Tomáš Paulus
Project Manager: Lea Petrášová
Developers: Jiří Lafek, Vašek Slavík

Old version problems

  • Outdated UI and overall problems with usability and understandability
  • Lots of hidden features that prevented seamless UX
  • Bad designed information architecture

Redesign goals

  • Run user tests, check analytics and make heuristic evaluation of the app
  • Refresh UI but keep it calm, design new typography and color schemes
  • Make it more intuitive and easy to understand

Our process

Research

  • Heuristic evaluation
  • Competitive analysis
  • HotJar and Google Analytics review
  • Guerilla testing

Redesign

  • Data architecture

Visuals and testing

Original design of platform

First version of crypto binary trading platform was designed in 2016 without research or any user tests. Whole platform has been developed without further assistance of UI/UX designers so we had a lot of space for creativity and research based design.

Visual and testing

After initial research, competitive analysis and guerilla testing we re-created whole sitemap, information architecture and on-boarding flow. The next logical step was to create new style-guide and refresh platform for year 2018.

Refreshed visuals

Our team tested 4 colour schemes for light version and 2 schemes for a dark version of the app. The final design can be seen down below. We used a lot of negative space, bigger fonts, brighter colours and we tried to highlight the most important information for users.

Testing and clickable prototypes

Qualitative testing

We tested the app with users who are regular traders and also with people who just want to try binary trading. After the initial set of subjects, we did a lot of changes to make the platform even more usable.

Clickable prototypes

Our UX Designer created clickable prototypes in Figma. These prototypes were used by developers to understand the interactivity of the trading platform. We also used these prototypes for user testing before implementing visual changes to the live app.
