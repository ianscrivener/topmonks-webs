What are we working on?

Variability is our specialty. Whether it’s B2B, startup or blockchain, we choose meaningful projects with a modern tech stack.

Bigger companies

We are not an agency. We prefer to deliver complete custom solutions rather than people. We transform the processes & structures of our B2B clients into lovable products. Of course, remotely, if that’s your preference.

Startups

Working with our startup crew will open your mind! Welcome to the world of innovative ideas, decentralized freedom and latest technologies. We support our clients in converting thoughts into real, user-friendly products.

Blockchain projects

TopMonks is a decentralized company. What does it mean? We transmute our passion for blockchain into a fraction of our team. Our Blockchain Studio. Working on top-notch projects, starting with UX, ending with complex backend development.

Everyday life in TopMonks

We don't need you messing around from 9 to 5 in the office. We want to deliver perfection! As our team is mostly distributed, we've worked diligently to improve the ways we work together, and with our clients. Don't count hours, deliver!

Slack

Effective communication is the essence of cooperation - across all of our projects, and with all of our customers. We don't really care where you sit, but once you are working, you should be available.

Github

All our projects are running in GitHub. Many of them also use GitHub Issues for tracking the progress. Together with Slack it is the alpha and omega of our cooperation.

Swarmpit

Most of our projects are containerized and run in Docker Swarm environment. For management of these we developed our own UI called Swarmpit.

Quip

Here we share all the projects documentation, invoices, even our company library, basically our whole knowledge. Ask Rarous for his amazing cook book!
